Hey, Creative Coder

Put your programming skills to work and create amazing theater with us !

The Great Outdoors

Here's your chance to go beyond those 'interesting' creative coding challenges. We invite you to work with us on a bigger canvas. A chance to collaborate with some very well known creatives in this space and create something that really affects people.

About you

Python programming and good communication skills are essential for this role

Experience with machine learning, generative art, text processing, big data processing, or Apache Spark is a plus. However, if you don't have that extra experience this project will give you a chance to stretch your programming wings and learn more about those things in a supportive environment.

You will be working with a codebase (and a data pipeline) already under active development. Your task will be to put this into context and to realise and respond to ideas in the theater context. At times you will be the only coder on set and will need to be flexible and able to translate concept level art into coding changes on the fly.

About the project

If you are interested in text, theater and creativity we this is an amazing opportunity.

We have funding and will consider profit sharing initiatives as well as a stipend for your work.
